Tribute Page to Gustavo Faria

Gustavo Faria is a Brazilian software developer, project manager, team leader, geek, podcaster, writer, blogger, youtuber, coacher and Apple Evangelist. Graduated on information technology by Federal University of Rio de Janeiro in 1995, having more than 25 years of experience on technology.

His work:

His major works are CocaTech, Mestre do Mac (Master of Mac), Hackeando a Vida (Life Hack) and Hackeando a Produtividade (Life Hack). His work is in Portuguese, but you can give it a try if you don’t speak Portuguese.

CocaTech

CocaTech is a Brazilian technology hub. In this ecosystem you can find audios, texts and videos. Besides technology, the hub brings information about news, APPs tips, self-improvement, home and life hacks and more. The CocaTech’s Podcast can be considering the icing on the cake. On its seventh season and delivered tree times per day (awesome!), the podcast is a good companion for your daily routine. Additionally, the hub has a YouTube Channel, a blog, and more.

Mestre do Mac

Mestre do Mac is a book that has 10 tips to improve your knowledge about your Mac and the Mac environment. It’s free!

Hackeando a Vida

Hackeando a Vida is a projects that helps you make your life easier.

Hackeando a Produtividade

Hackeando a Produtividade is a project that helps you improve your productivity.
